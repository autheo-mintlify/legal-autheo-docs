title: 'Exhibit G — Hosting and delegation policy'
---

*Version 1.0 — Effective November 20, 2025*

## Scope
Describes self-hosting vs. managed hosting options for Sovereign, Prime, and Core validator licenses.

## Approved providers
Autheo may publish a non-exhaustive list (e.g., Zeeve, InfStones). Listing is informational and may change without notice.

## Costs
Hosting fees are paid by the license holder directly to the provider. Any fee ranges published by Autheo are illustrative and not quotes.

## Onboarding
Autheo will publish non-binding specs and runbooks for self-hosting; these do not modify Exhibit B.

## Compliance
KYC/OFAC and jurisdictional restrictions may apply to hosting choices and payout routing.

## Security and keys
Purchaser retains sole responsibility for key custody and validator safety (even when using a provider).

## Service changes
Providers may change pricing/terms; Purchaser is responsible for monitoring, renewals, and migrations.
